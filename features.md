## Core Features

- **Powerful card search** — Filters across all cards and sets, fully offline
- **Card scanner** — Scan cards with the camera (artwork-based recognition)
- **Live pricing** — Up-to-date prices from TCGplayer, Card Kingdom, Star City Games, Cardmarket, Cardhoarder, Mana Pool
- **Deck builder** — Check deck value, view Mana Curve, Mana Production stats
- **Collection manager** — Organize cards in sorted binders and lists
- **Deck simulator** — Test and improve decks with a powerful simulator
- **Card information** — Complete card info with up-to-date rules and legalities
- **Deck sharing** — Share decks via links openable in any browser
- **Wishlist / Favorites** — Track favorite cards
- **MTG news feed** — Articles from multiple Magic: The Gathering sources
- **Trade tool** — Faster, fairer trades; search by set and card version

## Full Description

ManaBox is an unofficial companion tool for Magic: The Gathering (MTG) players. With ManaBox you can search through all cards and sets without exception for FREE. ManaBox allows you to access up to date market data from different stores and marketplaces so you always know the value of your cards or look at the prices of the cards you would like to get.

Easily digitalize your collection with the built-in card scanner and have it available in your pocket at all times.

Keep all your decks organized inside the app and place them in folders if you prefer. You can also share links to them that can be opened in any browser.

You can share any card you want with your friends as well as a link to the marketplace of your choice.

View any set and any card in MTG history, all in one app. The always up-to-date database means you will never miss any recently released set or card.

ManaBox includes a powerful trade tool that allows you to do better trades, faster and fairer. Search easily between different sets and choose the specific card version you want to trade.

## User Feedback Highlights

**Positive:**
- Best app for managing a collection
- Ability to share deck links is well-liked
- Cross-deck card search is valued

**Negative / Requested Improvements:**
- Card scanner can misidentify the set for older cards (workaround: lock sets in scanner settings)
- Subscription model is a friction point — some users want a one-time purchase option
- Scanner can become laggy/choppy after certain updates
